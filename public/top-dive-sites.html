The Best DiveSites WorldWide - according to Marta

My top Divesites of all-dives.

Pass St.Jacques

Mauritius, Le Morne

I love it because of variety of mrine life, and unique character - you start inside the lagoon, and drift out of it with a current.

  • max depth: 30+ meters
  • inaxessible during windy weather if winds from south, south-east
  • you dive a reverse profile - start shallow and end up deeper in the second part of the dive
  • high probability of turtle, eagle ray and reef shark encounters

Astron Wreck

Dominican Republic, Bavaro

Shalow dive site, with good weather for all experience levels. When the visibility is good (usually summer months) offers stunning views of the wreck and it's huge propeller.

  • max depth 15 meters - abd you need to dig to get to this
  • not too much in terms of marine life, but the wreck is amazing
  • penetration of main chamber only possible on realy calm days - therwise the water movement can be too intense
  • typical marine life includes parrot fish, wrasse, morrays

Santo Antao

Cape Verde, Sal Island

The home of hundrets of puffer fish. It is literally impossible to count them on a single dive.

  • max depth 14 meters
  • construction is becoming more and more unstable due to water movements - swimming through not advisable
  • home to several species of uffer and porcupine fish

The Wall of Catalina Island

Dominican Republic, Catalina, Bayahibe

Lovely carribean vibes ans abundance of marine live and corals.

  • max depth - over 60 meters
  • tricky start in the shallow reef part - with boat traffic overhead
  • sometimes quite a current along the wall - be careful to start agiants it
  • have a good look out for lobsters on the wall
